Home Safety

Fire safety!
A simple fact: the best way to prevent injury and death from house fires is to have smoke alarms installed and working properly. It is often children who are the tragic victims of house fires.

Fire Safety Risk Assessment of Workplace Facilities: A Case Study

Workplace facilities are organizational capital-intensive, which entail high risks of fire occurrence. The fire risks increase based on occupant's behaviors, lack of awareness and poor workplace safety management. Thus, fire safety risk assessment is vital to raise awareness about workplace fire-safety culture.

Fire Safety Precautions

Good life safety practices that you should follow include:
- Keeping your workplace tidy and having a good standard of housekeeping
- Regularly removing combustible waste, including dust
- Keeping ignition sources away from combustible material
